PRE-MARRIAGE COUNSELLING

Let's be real, you’ve thought about this at least once (ok, twice) which is totally normal because what person doesn't want a long-lasting, fulfilling and blissful life with their partner?

The fact is, so much thought, energy and planning go into the actual wedding - but it’s equally important to give your marriage the same level of consideration to be sure you’ve set a solid foundation as you enter the next stage of your relationship.

This is where a Pre Marriage Counselling course comes in.

The purpose of Pre Marriage Counselling is to create a safe space for you and your partner to talk, as well as establish a partnership where you both feel seen, honoured and valued.

It’s a bonding experience that not only develops better understanding, but enables you to listen, support and negotiate with each other in a positive and productive way.

There’s always room to learn more about ourselves and our partners and doing a pre wedding course shows a willingness to improve, develop and commit to each other.

As a relationship specialist trained in couples therapy, I offer a customised Pre Marriage Counselling course that helps you build a solid foundation for a powerful, fulfilling and intimate life-long relationship together.
